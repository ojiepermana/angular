## Design Context

### Users

Primary users are Angular teams and library maintainers who need a standard, reusable foundation for theme, layout, and UI primitives. They use this library to assemble consistent application shells and shared components without rebuilding the same patterns in each project.

### Brand Personality

The brand should feel futuristic, clean, and clear. The interface should project confidence and professionalism first, with enough energy to feel current and forward-looking rather than conservative or flat.

### Aesthetic Direction

The visual direction should take cues from UniFi UI: structured, polished, and system-driven, with strong visual discipline and consistent alignment. Blue is the primary anchor color. Future design work should support both light and dark themes equally well, avoid messy or dirty surfaces, and reject asymmetrical or inconsistent compositions.

### Design Principles

- Build reusable UI primitives that feel standardized, not improvised.
- Keep layouts crisp, balanced, and highly consistent across screens and states.
- Use blue as the visual anchor, with restrained accents and strong contrast in both themes.
- Favor a futuristic product aesthetic through precision, clarity, and deliberate motion rather than ornament.
- Maintain WCAG AA accessibility, clear interaction states, and accommodations for inclusive use.
